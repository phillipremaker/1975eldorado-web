ELVIS 75

I owned a 1975 Cadillac Eldorado convertible from 2002 until 2010. I hope to someday own one again. When I owned it, I hand built a Web 1.0 site, and discovered a commmunity of owners and an amazing Yahoo mailing list. This website is a collection of my personal experience, stories, and some amalgamations of wisdom that I found useful. I converted it to a modern responsive website using Hugo and added https and IPv6 to make search engines like the content better. Let’s see what happens.

The survival guide in the Maintenance area is a good place to start is the best place to start.

You can reach me at eldo@remaker.com with questions or comments.
